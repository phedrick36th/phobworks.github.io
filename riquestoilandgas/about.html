About Us

Riquest Oil & Gas Limited is a wholly owned indigenous integrated petroleum marketing and logistics company, incorporated in November 2007. Riquest Oil & Gas Limited has grown to be the dominant player in the downstream sector of the Oil & Gas industry in the southern region of Nigerian.

We are renowned for offering consumers a variety of quality products from the oil value chain in a prompt and efficient manner.

Our product range includes Premium Motor Spirit (PMS), Automotive Gas Oil (AGO also known as Diesel), Dual Purpose Kerosene (DPK) and Liquified Petroleum Gas (LPG). We also offer Petroleum Products Transportation Services and Petroleum Logistics Consulting.
