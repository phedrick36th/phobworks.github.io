About Us

OSCORP GLOBAL was established in 2010, with a base in Port Harcourt, Rivers state, Nigeria.

The Company was registered as an Oil & Gas Servicing Company with emphasis on leasing of Marine and Land Equipment. With its oﬃce currently situated at House 1, Block 3, 2nd Avenue, Godwin Abbey Street, Elekahia Housing Estate, Port Harcourt, Rivers State and a Jetty Front at Abuloma Jetty which houses most of the Marine equipment. It’s well placed to serve the Industry.

OSCORP also has a jetty front at Ikonibo jetty where it carries out dry docking and maintenance of Sea Going equipment and vessels.

On inception, with a staﬀ strength of just 5 personnel and a single 400 ton dump barge, the company has grown to a total strength of over 30 key personnel and over 20 diﬀerent marine and land equipment, OSCORP GLOBAL story has been one of Grass to Grace.
